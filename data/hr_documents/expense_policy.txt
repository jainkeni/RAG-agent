EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
=============================

1. ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
- Travel (flights, trains, taxis)
- Accommodation during travel
- Meals during travel
- Office supplies for approved projects
- Client entertainment (with approval)

2. NON-ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
- Personal shopping
- Entertainment (movies, concerts)
- Alcoholic beverages
- Fitness/gym membership
- Car washing/maintenance

3. CLAIM PROCESS
- Submit within 30 days with original receipts
- Fill expense form with manager approval
- Process time: 7-10 working days
- Amount: Up to ₹50,000 per claim

4. SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS
- Original receipts/invoices
- Travel details (dates, purpose)
- Manager approval
- Attendee list (for group expenses)

5. HOTEL GUIDELINES
- Standard rooms only
- Max ₹5,000 per night (within India)
- Max ₹10,000 per night (outside India)
- Breakfast included in hotel charges

6. MEAL ALLOWANCE
- Breakfast: ₹500
- Lunch: ₹800
- Dinner: ₹800
- Combined daily max: ₹2,000
