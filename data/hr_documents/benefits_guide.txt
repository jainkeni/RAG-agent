EMPLOYEE BENEFITS GUIDE 2026
============================

1. HEALTH INSURANCE
- Coverage: Employee + Family (up to 4 members)
- Annual coverage: ₹10,00,000
- OPD coverage: ₹50,000 per year
- Eligibility: From day 1 of employment

2. PROVIDENT FUND (PF)
- Employee contribution: 12% of basic salary
- Employer contribution: 12% of basic salary
- Eligibility: All employees
- Can be withdrawn after 5 years of service

3. GRATUITY
- Applicable: After 5 years of continuous service
- Amount: (Last salary × Tenure in years × 15) / 30
- Upon resignation or retirement

4. PERFORMANCE BONUS
- Eligible: All permanent employees
- Amount: Up to 3 months salary based on performance
- Disbursed: March-April

5. TRAVEL ASSISTANCE
- Travel allowance for official trips
- Reimbursement within 7 days of submission
- Requires bills/receipts

6. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
- ₹50,000 per year for learning/certifications
- Requires prior approval
