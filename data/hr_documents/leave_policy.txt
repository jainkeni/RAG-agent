LEAVE POLICY 2026
=================

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
- Eligible employees: 20 days per year
- New employees (< 1 year): 12 days
- Carryover: Up to 5 days can be carried forward to next year
- Encashment: Unused leaves can be encashed at year-end

2. SICK LEAVE
- 10 days per year
- Requires medical certificate for absences > 3 consecutive days
- Cannot be carried forward

3. CASUAL LEAVE
- 3 days per year
- Minimum 1 day notice required
- Not applicable for weekends/holidays

4. MATERNITY LEAVE
- 6 months fully paid (for female employees)
- Additional 6 months unpaid if required
- Available for all female employees

5. LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
- Submit leave request at least 7 days in advance
- Get approval from reporting manager
- In emergency, notify HR immediately
- Approval via email within 24 hours

6. GENERAL RULES
- Leave cannot be applied for past dates
- Leaves during notice period are restricted
- Cannot defer more than 5 days
- Public holidays are not counted as leave
